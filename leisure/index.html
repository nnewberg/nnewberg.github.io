There once was a place hidden deep under the sea, where creatures large and small sought refuge from the ocean’s tempestuous waves. Mermaids, pirates, and Polynesian princes all gathered there to share in exotic libations, turning even the fiercest enemies into lasting friends.For years, many believed that this place was lost forever after a great storm sent it down into the farthest reaches of the ocean’s depths. But one day, a brave sea explorer saw a bright light emanating from the ocean floor and knew in that moment that the enchanted place was lost no more. The explorer quickly brought his discovery ashore and has kept it hidden in plain sight ever since, cloaked in the guise of our everyday world. Few have been lucky enough to be granted access, but today, you have been chosen to join us at Tiki Mirage.
